Twitter Archive

Tuesday, 20 December 2022 17:00 +0100

I’ve created an online archive of my Twitter content, using Tweetback, a tool that turns your exported data into a self-contained, searchable, nicely rendered repository suitable for inclusion on your own site. Probably useful mostly to myself, as I often find myself remembering that I linked to something in a Tweet rather than the exact details. It also feels good to own this data again, no matter how irrelevant most it may seem.
